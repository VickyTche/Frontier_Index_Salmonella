# Frontier_Index_Salmonella
Jupyter notebook file for the determination of the Frontier Index from Hi-C contact maps of Salmonella enterica serovar Typhimurium strain SL1344. 
Data used:
EEP: Hi-C of WT S. Typhimurium SL1344 cells growing in LB at 37°C OD=0,1 (EEP condition) 
HilD: Hi-C of ΔhilD  S. Typhimurium SL1344 cells growing in LB at 37°C OD=2 (ESP condition) 
MKL82_OFF: Hi-C of WT S. Typhimurium SL1344 cells growing in LB at 37°C OD=2 (ESP GFP- condition)
MKL81_ON: Hi-C of WT S. Typhimurium SL1344 cells growing in LB at 37°C OD=2 (ESP GFP+ condition) 
MKL41: Hi-C of ΔhilE  S. Typhimurium SL1344 cells growing in LB at 37°C OD=2 (ESP GFP+ condition)
MKL42: Hi-C of ΔhilE  S. Typhimurium SL1344 cells growing in LB at 37°C OD=2 (ESP GFP- condition)

These normalized matrices have been deposited in the NCBI Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO, https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/) under the accession code GSE289504.

Bacterial chromatin remodeling associated with transcription-induced domains at pathogenicity Islands
